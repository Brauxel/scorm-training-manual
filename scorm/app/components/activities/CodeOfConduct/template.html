Code of conduct

You need to comply with your employer’s code of conduct (covering drugs and alcohol use etc.) and work health and safety policies and procedures.

Social media

Want to express yourself and communicate through social media like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, blogs etc.? That’s okay, but bear in mind:

  • the internet is not an anonymous tool
  • use good judgement about what you post, especially if the material could potentially damage the reputation of nbn, its customers, its products, its suppliers or its people
  • comply with the social media policy of both nbn and your employer.
