More about brands
So, what’s a brand?

A brand is more than just a logo. A brand offers a promise.

When you see these well known brands, you instantly know what they sell and what value they offer you.

Every time you buy one of those brands you understand the type of product you’ll be getting, and you expect a consistent experience from them.

You also know how you feel about a brand when it doesn’t live up to the promises they make.
